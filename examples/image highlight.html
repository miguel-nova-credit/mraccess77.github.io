GitHub Copilot: Web accessibility refers to the inclusive practice of ensuring there are no barriers that prevent interaction with, or access to, websites on the World Wide Web by people with physical disabilities, situational disabilities, and socio-economic restrictions on bandwidth. When sites are correctly designed, developed and edited, generally all users have equal access to information and functionality. It also benefits people without disabilities, for example mobile phone users.
